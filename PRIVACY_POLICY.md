# Privacy Policy for in-browser-design-llm

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the "in-browser-design-llm" Chrome extension.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The extension collects and uses the following information:
- **Google Gemini API Key**: This key is required to perform design modifications using Google's Gemini models.

## 2. Data Storage and Transmission

- **Local Storage**: Your Gemini API key is stored only in your browser's local storage (Chrome Sync Storage). It is never sent to our servers or any third parties other than Google.
- **Direct Communication with Google API**: When you use the extension, the API key is sent directly from your browser to the Google Gemini API (https://generativelanguage.googleapis.com) to process your requests.
- **No Server-Side Storage**: We do not maintain any servers that store your personal data, API keys, or browsing history.

## 3. Third-Party Services

This extension uses the Google Gemini API. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for information on how Google handles data sent to its APIs.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/igtm/in-browser-design-llm
